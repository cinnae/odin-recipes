Mince meat quesadillas

Back


8 servings || Adapted/originally from K-ruoka

One in the many lines of untested recipes.

  • Pack of mince meat
  • Can of salsa dip
  • Can of cheese dip
  • Pack of shredded cheese
  • 8 flour tortillas
  1. Brown the mince. Add salsa dip, wait for 5 minutes, add a pinch of salt.
  2. Heat up some butter, add in a tortilla. For one half add cheese dip, mince and shredded cheese.
  3. Flip the other half over it and wait for a few minutes for the cheese to melt.
  4. Cut to sectors if you cba.
