Halloumicurry

Back


Serves approx. five people || Adapted/originally from IS.fi

The simplified version, because there's way too many ingredients and steps in the original.
Never tried making it, so who knows how it would actually taste like?

  • Two packs of halloumi cheese (400g)
  • Can of chickpeas (400g)
  • Pack of frozen bell peppers
  • Pack of frozen carrots
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • Can of tomato paste
  • Can of coconut cream
  • Salt, chili/cayenne, garlic, cumin, turmeric, paprika
  • Ginger & parsley optional
  1. Cut up the cheese, toss it on the pan with the oil.
  2. Add the peppers and carrots. Add in the spices.
  3. Add the chickpeas, tomatoes, coconut cream and 1dl water.
  4. Mix and leave for about half an hour. Serve with rice.
  5. Add parsley at the end if you have it, but really - who cares?
